Program Overview

Our 7-month AI-Empowered Software Engineer Bootcamp is designed for absolute beginners. You'll master modern web development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, TypeScript) and learn to leverage the latest AI coding tools—like Cursor, Copilot, Windsurf, and Claude Code—to accelerate your software engineering workflow. Career coaching and professional development are embedded throughout the program. The final phase is a short, inspirational introduction to AI engineering possibilities and career paths.

Project-Based Learning Journey

Throughout the program, you'll build 4 progressive projects: a personal portfolio, a task management app, an AI-enhanced e-commerce platform, and your custom capstone application. Each project builds on previous skills while introducing new AI-powered development techniques using tools like Cursor, Windsurf, Copilot, and LLM APIs. Your final portfolio will showcase real-world applications that demonstrate your mastery to potential employers.
